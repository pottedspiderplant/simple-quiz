# Canadian geography

What types of trees are found in boreal forests?
- Pines, spruces and larches

What's another name for a boreal forest?
- Taiga

What is the Canadian Shield?
- A geologic shield, a large area of exposed Precambrian igneous and high-grade metamorphic rocks.

Name the Great Lakes.
- Lake Huron, Lake Erie, Lake Ontario, Lake Michigan, and Lake Superior.

What is the largest Great Lake?
- Lake Superior

What is the smallest Great Lake?
- Lake Ontario

What are the physiographic regions of Canada?
- The Canadian Shield, the interior plains, the Great Lakes (St. Lawrence Lowlands), the Appalachian region, the Western Cordillera, Hudson Bay Lowlands and the Arctic Archipelago.
